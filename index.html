Self-Care Tips

  • Practice mindfulness through deep breathing exercises.
  • Stay physically active to reduce stress and anxiety.
  • Try journaling or writing about your emotions.
  • Reach out to friends, family, or a counselor when you feel overwhelmed.
  • Make time for hobbies or activities that bring you joy.
